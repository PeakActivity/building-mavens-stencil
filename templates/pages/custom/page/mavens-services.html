We offer a variety of specialized engineering services including:

Milestone Inspections

We work in compliance with the Florida Statutes (553.899). This law requires statewide structural inspections for aging condominiums and cooperative buildings that are three stories or more in height.

Structural Integrity Reserve Studies (SIRS)

Florida Statutes (718.112 and 719.106) require condominium and cooperative buildings that are three stories or taller to have and maintain a study of reserve funds. This is necessary for any future major repairs and replacements of their common elements. Our team is equipped to perform these studies prior to the turnover of properties to unit owners.

Building Safety and Recertification

Allow our team to handle your mandatory structural and electrical safety inspections for non-exempt buildings located in Broward and Miami-Dade counties.

Construction Litigation and Expert Witness

We are capable of providing full-service construction defect inspections, reporting, litigation support, and expert witness testimony.

Property Turnover And Condition Assessments

Whether you are a developer, association, or owner, our team will inspect your real estate asset during a transfer, turnover, or capital improvements planning. We can also perform this task simply for your peace of mind.

Infrared Thermography Inspection Services

An infrared thermography inspection utilizes an approved infrared imager, operated by a qualified technician, to scan and capture video or still infrared images of electrical components.

Commercial sUASDrone Inspections

This service offers 360-degree aerial inspections through a Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems (sUAS) drone operated by licensed and highly-skilled pilots. With its spherical capture feature, it provides high-resolution and thermal imagery.
