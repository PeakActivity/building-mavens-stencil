Scott Harvey-Lewis is a forensic engineer with over 17 years of industry experience. He has worked on several public and private-sector projects, providing structural engineering design, construction administration, and forensic services. He investigates structural failures, construction defects, and code compliance of a project on behalf of our client. His work ensures that a project remains on schedule and, more importantly, complies with the required safety standards and building codes. Scott is also a skilled design engineer whose expertise includes building envelope systems and cast-in-place concrete, steel, masonry, and timber designs. He has made designs for residential and commercial structures such as warehouses and piers. His design portfolio includes significant phases of project development, from conceptual design and plan preparation to construction and final inspection.
